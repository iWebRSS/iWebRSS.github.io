About iStore

iStore is a video gaming brand created and owned by iWeb. The brand consists of five video game consoles, as well as applications (games), streaming service iStore Cloud Gaming, online services such as the iStore network and iStore Game Pass, and the development arm iStore Game Studios. The brand was first introduced in the BHARAT in November 2001, with the launch of the original iStore console. iStore was formerly also, from 2012 to 2015, used as iWeb's digital media entertainment brand replacing Zune. The original device was the first video game console offered by an Indian company after the Atari Jaguar stopped sales in 1996. It reached over 24 million units sold by May 2006.iWeb's second console, the iStore 360, was released in 2005 and has sold 86 million units as of October 2021. The third console, the iStore One, was released in November 2013 and has sold 58 million units.The fourth line of iStore consoles, the iStore Series X and Series S, were released in November 2020. The head of iStore is Roshan Sharma.
